Mental Health Resources

Practical tools, tips, and exercises to help you manage stress, anxiety, and depression

Grounding Techniques

Use these when feeling overwhelmed or anxious:

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Method: Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Deep Breathing: Inhale 4 sec, hold 4 sec, exhale 6 sec
  • Touch something cold or textured to reconnect with the present

Daily Mood Tracker

Track your emotions to identify patterns and triggers:

  • Rate your mood 1–10 each morning and night
  • Note what influenced your mood (sleep, events, people)
  • Review weekly to spot trends and coping successes

Self-Care Checklist

Simple habits to support mental wellness:

  • Drink enough water and eat nourishing meals
  • Move your body for 20+ minutes daily
  • Sleep 7–9 hours and maintain a routine
  • Set 1 boundary to protect your energy

Helpful Apps & Websites

Free tools to support your journey:

  • Headspace or Calm — for guided meditation
  • Moodfit or Daylio — for mood tracking
  • NAMI.org — National Alliance on Mental Illness
  • 7 Cups — free peer support chats
